Total FOV: 200°

Binocular Vision

Binocular vision allows both eyes to work together, providing depth perception and a wider field of view.

Advantages:

  • Better depth perception.
  • Reduced blind spots.
  • Improved motion detection.

Monocular Vision

Monocular vision occurs when only one eye is used at a time, common in animals with side-positioned eyes.

Advantages:

  • Wider peripheral vision.
  • Sharper focus on distant objects.
